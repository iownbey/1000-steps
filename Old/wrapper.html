Escape the Dungeon!

Aragore the dragon has locked you in his nefarious dungeon. Only by fighting your way out of this labyrinth will you be able to escape. "Magics" and "Talk" do different things to different monsters. "Defend" will block the next attack, but it cannot be used multiple times in a row. "Heal" allows you to recover, but its effect diminishes the more it is used in a row. Press "Walk" to start your adventure!
